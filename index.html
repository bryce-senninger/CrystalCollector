Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the provided number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the provided number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, you'll have to click it to know!

Each time the game starts, the values given to each crystal will change.
